About the Platform

NADI — Navigating Assets, Dependents and Financial Insights

Platform Overview

NADI is a digital educational platform developed by the Faculty of Law (FUU), Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM). The platform assists users in assessing their family's financial readiness through a structured five-step assessment covering family structure, income sources, assets, liabilities, financial protection and financial continuity planning.

Educational Purpose

The application is designed to support authentic learning in Islamic Banking and Takaful education. It provides a practical, hands-on environment for students to understand real-world financial planning concepts within an Islamic finance framework, including Takaful, Hibah and Wasiat.

NADI Readiness Index

NADI generates a proprietary Family Financial Readiness Index scored from 0 to 100, categorised as Critical, Vulnerable, Developing or Resilient. The index is computed from five analytical dimensions: Family Dependency, Financial Risk, Protection Adequacy, Financial Continuity and Wealth Preservation.

Disclaimer

This platform is intended solely for educational and learning purposes. The results, scores and recommendations generated by NADI do not constitute financial, legal, Takaful or investment advice. Users are encouraged to consult qualified professionals for personalised financial guidance.
